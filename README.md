# Billing & Customer Management System

A modern web-based billing and customer management system for tracking orders, payments, and customer information.
https://rathan-mc.github.io/Billing-and-Customer-Management-System/

## Features

- **Dashboard Overview**: Real-time display of total paid and due amounts
- **Record Management**: Add, view, and delete billing records
- **Customer Information**: Track customer names, dates, and order details
- **Product Details**: Manage quantity (kg) and cover sizes with dropdown selection
- **Payment Tracking**: Monitor payment status (Paid/Due) and payment modes
- **Manufacturer Selection**: Choose between Gujarat and Hyderabad manufacturers
- **Data Export**: Export records to CSV format
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and mobile devices
- **Local Storage**: Data persists in browser storage

## Cover Sizes Available

- 5x8, 8x18, 9x13, 10x12, 11x14, 13x16, 16x20, 17x23, 20x26, 24x30

## Manufacturers

- Gujarat
- Hyderabad

## Payment Modes

- Online
- Cash

## Usage

1. **Adding Records**: Fill out the form with customer details and click "Add Record"
2. **Viewing Totals**: Check the summary cards at the top for paid and due amounts
3. **Filtering**: Use the dropdown to filter records by payment status
4. **Exporting**: Click "Export Data" to download records as CSV
5. **Deleting**: Use the delete button in each record row
